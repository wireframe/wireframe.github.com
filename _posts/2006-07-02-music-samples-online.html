I finished adding two new pieces to my website this weekend.



First, I integrated my delicious bookmarks using the linkroll javascript posted on delicious. It's a very robust API with lots of options (specific tags, restrict number of results, etc), and has very good CSS options as well.



And I finally uploaded some sample music from the drifting North CD. I've been meaning to do this for a long time, and I figured now was a good time to try out Audacity. Now, I've just got to figure out a distribution channel for selling CD's. For now, anyone interested can contact me directly, and I'll get one in the mail ASAP. I was pretty impressed that delicious even offers some custom javascript for playing mp3's on your site as well!



Just a few more signs that you can pull off some cool dynamic content for static sites.
